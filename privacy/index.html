Privacy Notice (GDPR)

Fae is a privacy-focused local app. After install and startup, Fae runs on your computer and keeps your data on your device.

Effective date: February 2026. Last updated: February 2026.

1. GDPR Summary

Fae is designed so that Saorsa Labs does not receive your conversations, memories, voice data, camera data, prompts, or files from the app. Processing happens locally on your machine.

Under GDPR principles (data minimisation and privacy by design), Fae is built to avoid collecting personal data in the first place for normal app usage.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect telemetry, analytics, tracking data, or behavioral data from the Fae app. We do not run cloud inference for your local conversations.

  • No app telemetry
  • No ad tracking
  • No profiling
  • No sale of personal data

3. Local-First Companion

Fae is your local AI companion. Once installed, it runs on your computer, uses local models, and stores its working data locally. You can inspect, export, or delete local files under your own system account.
